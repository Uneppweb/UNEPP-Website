Specialized Offshore Engineering Services

UNEPP has developed robust capabilities in offshore engineering, with an annual average of 150,000 man-hours dedicated to Greenfield and Brownfield offshore projects. Our specialized experience spans across Front-End Engineering Design (FEED), detailed engineering, offshore installation analysis, and transportation studies—supporting clients in achieving safe, efficient, and cost-effective marine operations.

Structural DesignComprehensive FEED and detailed design of offshore structures, supported by non-linear boat impact simulations and advanced SACS modeling encompassing static and dynamic in-place analysis, seismic and pushover assessments, and spectral fatigue life evaluation.
Installation EngineeringPile drivability analyses, comprehensive sea transportation engineering, offshore installation structural analyses, ballast and towing evaluations, design and certification of barge modifications and buoyancy tanks, and preparation of detailed Installation Manuals with activity checklists.
Marine OperationsFull-scope engineering from load-out through installation, including rigging and lift point design, dropped object studies, jacket and subsea structure installation, subsea systems, float-over engineering with hydrostatic/hydrodynamic and DP capability assessments, and comprehensive support for floating units covering temporary phases, tow and hook-up, and mooring line change-out.
