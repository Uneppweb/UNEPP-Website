Comprehensive services across the full project lifecycle

Feasibility Studies and Concept Selection

We provide Engineering Studies and Concept Selection services to support informed, risk-mitigated, and cost-effective decisions during the early phases of project development—aligned with technical, operational, and regulatory requirements across oil & gas, petrochemical, and infrastructure sectors.

Process Simulation & Preliminary DesignValidation of key assumptions to support early-stage decisions.

Approach

Specialized Offshore Engineering Services

UNEPP maintains robust offshore engineering capabilities with an annual average of 150,000 man-hours dedicated to Greenfield and Brownfield offshore projects—covering FEED, detailed engineering, installation analysis, and transportation studies.

Structural Design and Analysis • FEED and detailed design for offshore structures (boat landings, riser/conductor protection)
• Non-linear boat impact simulations
• Advanced SACS modeling: in-place static/dynamic, seismic & pushover, spectral fatigue life
Offshore Installation Engineering • Pile drivability (GRLWEAP)
• Sea transportation: routing, stability, motion, cribbing, sea-fastening, metocean
• Lifting, flotation, launch, upending analysis; ballast/stability; bollard pull for towing
• Barge modifications and buoyancy tanks design & certification
• Installation manuals and activity checklists
Fabrication, Transportation & Installation Support Engineering from load-out (skidded, lifted, SPMT, float-on) to installation with complete documentation.
Subsea Systems Support Engineering inputs for mooring, SURF, power cables, and subsea installation activities.
Float-over & Floating Units Support Hydrostatic/hydrodynamic assessments, DP capability analysis, tow & hook-up, and mooring line change-out.
