Feasibility Studies and Concept Selection

We offer comprehensive Engineering Studies and Concept Selection services to support clients in making informed, risk-mitigated, and cost-effective decisions during early project development. Our capabilities are tailored to align with technical, operational, and regulatory requirements across oil & gas, petrochemical, and infrastructure sectors.

Feasibility StudiesAssess technical and economic viability of proposed developments.
Concept Screening & SelectionCompare alternatives based on performance, safety, operability, and lifecycle cost.
Technology AssessmentDefine fit-for-purpose solutions aligned with objectives and site conditions.
Process Simulation & Preliminary DesignValidate assumptions and support early-stage decision-making.
Pre-FEED DeliverablesDefine scope, execution strategies, and interface requirements.
Risk & Safety IntegrationIntegrate HAZID, ENVID, and preliminary QRA into concept evaluation.

Our approach emphasizes early identification of project risks and opportunities, enabling stakeholders to proceed with confidence into FEED and detailed design phases.

Specialized Offshore Engineering Services

UNEPP has developed robust offshore engineering capabilities with an annual average of 150,000 man-hours dedicated to Greenfield and Brownfield offshore projects. Our experience spans FEED, detailed engineering, offshore installation analysis, and transportation studies—supporting safe, efficient, and cost-effective marine operations.

Structural Design and Analysis • FEED and detailed design for offshore structures (boat landings, riser/conductor protection)
• Non-linear simulations of boat impacts
• Advanced SACS modeling: in-place static/dynamic, seismic & pushover, spectral fatigue life
Offshore Installation Engineering • Pile drivability analyses using GRLWEAP
• Sea transportation: routing, stability, motion, cribbing, sea-fastening, metocean
• Lifting/flotation/launch/upending analysis; ballast & stability; bollard pull for towing
• Design & certification of barge modifications and buoyancy tanks
• Installation manuals and activity checklists
Fabrication, Transportation & Installation Support Engineering from load-out (skidded, lifted, SPMT, float-on) to installation, including rigging, lift points, dropped object studies, and installation documentation.
Subsea Systems Support Engineering support for mooring, SURF, power cables, and subsea installation activities.
Float-over & Floating Units Support Float-over method engineering, DP capability assessments, tow & hook-up, and mooring line change-out support.
