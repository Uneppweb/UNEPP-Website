About Us

Company Overview

United Engineers for Petroleum Projects (UNEPP) is an independently registered company, established in Egypt in 2008. We deliver comprehensive technical services across all phases of oil and gas development — from concept to commissioning — built on a strong foundation of engineering excellence and reliable project execution.

To extend our regional presence across the MENA region, our UAE-registered sister company, Unified Engineering Consultancy Limited (UNIFIED), was independently established in 2023. Although UNEPP and UNIFIED are independently registered entities, they share the same group of shareholders and operate in close coordination.

Together, UNEPP and UNIFIED provide integrated engineering, procurement, and project management services across the full project lifecycle — including feasibility studies, concept selection, basic and detailed design, procurement support, construction supervision, commissioning, and operational support.

UNEPP has been officially registered with the Egyptian Natural Gas Holding Company (EGAS) since February 2009 (Registration No. 1258), and with the Egyptian General Petroleum Corporation (EGPC) since December 2010 (Registration No. 68).

Both UNEPP and UNIFIED are certified to ISO 9001 (Quality Management), ISO 14001 (Environmental Management), and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety), demonstrating our commitment to delivering services that meet the highest international standards.
