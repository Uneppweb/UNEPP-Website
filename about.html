About Us

Company Overview

United Engineers for Petroleum Projects (UNEPP) is an independently registered company, established in Egypt in 2008. We deliver comprehensive technical services across all phases of oil and gas development — from concept to commissioning — built on a strong foundation of engineering excellence and reliable project execution.

To extend our regional presence across the MENA region, our UAE-registered sister company, Unified Engineering Consultancy Limited (UNIFIED), was independently established in 2023.

Although UNEPP and UNIFIED are independently registered entities, they share the same group of shareholders and operate in close coordination. Together, they provide integrated engineering, procurement, and project management services across the full project lifecycle — including feasibility studies, concept selection, basic and detailed design, procurement support, construction supervision, commissioning, and operational support.

UNEPP has been officially registered with the Egyptian Natural Gas Holding Company (EGAS) since February 2009 under Registration No. 1258, and with the Egyptian General Petroleum Corporation (EGPC) since December 2010 under Registration No. 68.

Both UNEPP and UNIFIED are certified to ISO 9001 (Quality Management), ISO 14001 (Environmental Management), and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety), demonstrating our commitment to delivering services that meet the highest international standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Company Milestones

  • 2008 – Company Inception: UNEPP established in Egypt to deliver value-driven multidisciplinary solutions across oil & gas projects.
  • 2008 – First Feasibility & Basic Engineering: Techno-economic study for a 32” transmission pipeline (El Sadat – Dahshour) for TAQA.
  • 2009 – First Detailed Engineering Services: Firefighting network detailed engineering for RAS BAKR Oil Facilities (GPC).
  • 2009 – First Operating Procedures Manual: Start-up and operating procedures manual for WDDM Phase V (BURULLUS).
  • 2012 – First Pipeline Construction Project: Installation of a 16-inch, 40 km high-pressure underground gas pipeline for NAT Gas.
  • 2012 – First HAZOP Study: HAZOP study for South ANNAJMA EPF (ECG) for STAROIL.
  • 2015 – First Process Skids Fabrication: Fabrication of two knockout drum skids for WASCO’s El Basant Compression Station.
  • 2021 – First Offshore Platform Detailed Engineering: Full detailed engineering for Petrogulf’s offshore platform.

HSE and Quality

Our management systems reflect our commitment to safeguarding people, assets, and the environment, while delivering engineering services that meet the highest standards of reliability and client satisfaction.

HSE

Our HSE Policy reflects our unwavering commitment to safeguarding people, assets, and the environment by upholding the highest standards of health, safety, and environmental performance. Our HSE Management System ensures proactive risk management by setting clear objectives and integrating preventive controls throughout the project lifecycle. Implementation is achieved through structured procedures, comprehensive training, and consistent application of safe work practices. Continuous monitoring via audits, inspections, and reporting enables timely corrective actions and drives ongoing improvement. Regular management reviews reinforce leadership commitment, ensuring compliance and the setting of new performance goals. At its core, the system embraces continuous improvement by incorporating lessons learned, best practices, and stakeholder feedback to enhance safety, efficiency, and environmental outcomes.

Quality
