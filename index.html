United Engineers for Petroleum Projects (UNEPP) is an independent, privately owned company, established and registered in Egypt in 2008. We deliver comprehensive engineering and project services across all phases of oil & gas development — from concept to commissioning — built on a strong foundation of engineering excellence and reliable project execution.
