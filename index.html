United Engineers for Petroleum Projects (UNEPP) is an independent, privately owned company, established and registered in Egypt in 2008. We deliver comprehensive engineering and project services across all phases of oil & gas development — from concept to commissioning — built on a strong foundation of engineering excellence and reliable project execution.

Serving Egypt and the wider MENA region

To serve the wider MENA region, we established our UAE-registered sister company, UNIFIED, in 2023. Although UNEPP and UNIFIED are independently registered entities, they share the same group of shareholders and operate in close coordination — providing integrated engineering, procurement, and project management services across the full project lifecycle.
